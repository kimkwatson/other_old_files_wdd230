Site Name

Queen Creek Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about Queen Creek’s Chamber of Commerce. The Chamber seeks to build a strong community in the town of Queen Creek by supporting and strengthening its businesses. The site will serve to connect local business-owners with each other, as well as with the town’s council, residents, and visitors. Users will be able to join the chamber, keep up with events, read about the town's history, and explore what Queen Creek has to offer.
